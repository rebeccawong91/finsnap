iOS Auto-Shortcut
Set up once. After that, every payment screenshot you take will be automatically detected and sent to FinSnap for logging.
1
Save this page as a Home Screen app
In Safari, tap the Share button (box with arrow) → "Add to Home Screen" → tap Add. This creates a FinSnap icon on your iPhone.
2
Open the Shortcuts app
It's pre-installed on every iPhone. Search for "Shortcuts" in Spotlight if you can't find it.
3
Create a new Shortcut
Tap + → tap "Add Action" → search "Get Latest Screenshot" and add it.
4
Add "Open App" with FinSnap
Add another action → search "Open App" → choose FinSnap (the icon you added in step 1). Then add "Share" and share the screenshot with FinSnap.
5
Set Automation trigger
Go to the Automation tab → + → Personal Automation → scroll to "Screenshot" → enable it. Choose your shortcut and turn off "Ask Before Running" for fully automatic flow.
HOW IT WORKS AFTER SETUP
1. You pay at Maybank/TnG/GrabPay etc.
2. You screenshot the receipt
3. Shortcut fires automatically
4. FinSnap opens and AI scans the receipt
5. Transaction is logged instantly ✓
QUICK MANUAL TRIGGER
Prefer manual? After screenshotting your receipt, swipe left on the preview thumbnail in the bottom-right corner → tap Share → select FinSnap from the Share Sheet. Done in 2 taps.
